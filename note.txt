5. Prize Pool Structure
5.1 Prize Distribution Calculation
Total Prize Pool: 55% of total ticket sales

Distribution Breakdown:

Prize	Percentage	Winner Type	Description
1st Prize	30%	Single winner	Grand prize winner
2nd Prize	10%	Single winner	Runner-up
3rd Prize	7%	Single winner	Second runner-up
Lucky Draw/4th Prize	5%	Multiple winners	Users with matching last digit of ticket
Fortune Wheel/5th Prize	3%	Multiple winners	Users who purchased 50+ tickets
Total: 55% (30% + 10% + 7% + 5% + 3%)

5.2 Detailed Prize Structure
5.2.1 Grand Prize (1st Prize) - 30%
Winner: Single winner

Selection: Random draw from all entries

Prize amount: 30% of total ticket sales pool

Special features:

Featured winner announcement

Certificate of win

Public recognition (with consent)

5.2.2 Second Prize - 10%
Winner: Single winner

Selection: Random draw from remaining entries

Prize amount: 10% of total ticket sales pool

Special features: Runner-up recognition

5.2.3 Third Prize - 7%
Winner: Single winner

Selection: Random draw from remaining entries

Prize amount: 7% of total ticket sales pool

5.2.4 Lucky Draw Prize (4th Prize) - 5%
Winners: Multiple winners possible

Selection Criteria: Users whose ticket numbers match a specific digit

Prize amount: 5% of total ticket sales pool (distributed among winners)

Distribution: Equal share among all qualifying winners

Example: If the winning digit is "5", all tickets ending with "5" win

5.2.5 Fortune Wheel Prize (5th Prize) - 3%
Eligibility: Users who purchased 50 or more tickets

Winners: Multiple winners possible

Selection: Random draw from eligible users

Prize amount: 3% of total ticket sales pool (distributed among winners)

Purpose: Reward bulk purchasers and loyal customers
